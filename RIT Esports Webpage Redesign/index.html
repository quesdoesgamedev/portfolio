RIT Esports

The RIT Esports club is a student-run club at the Rochester Institute of Technology. Students involved in the club will play different video games in a competitive setting, at an intramural level and a collegiate level against other universities. The club, started back in 2016, has become one of the nation's largest and best collegiate esports programs. Mimicking not only professional-level team management, fully staffed by students, but also featuring broadcasting, community, development, and production departments, the RIT Esports club has grown bigger than many collegiate athletics programs. The club is populated with over 300 players, and more than 2,000 community members who do community events, fundraisers, LAN tournament events, and much more.

RIT Esports currently features 19 teams and squads to get involved in. These include all sorts of various genres, from strategy, to card games, to the most popular esports genre, first person shooters. Pretty much every major competitive game is involved, and students can start squads for a new game and potentially get it upgraded to a full team if it gets enough support behind it.
