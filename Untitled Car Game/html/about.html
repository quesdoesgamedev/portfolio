Untitled Car Game

I. High Concept

Untitle Car Game is an infinite runner where you have to dodge oncoming cars as your car gradually speeds up with your score. The goal of the game is to get the highest score possible.

II. Genre

The genres of the game would be: endless runner, racing

III. Platform

This game is intended for desktop only. It will utilize the keyboard so mobile would not be able to play it.

IV. Story

UCG does not have a story, as it is an abstract skateboarding game. The player motivation would be to get a high score.

V. Esthetics

The art style of this game is pixel art- I will make all the art and music assets (SFX sounds are from a royalty free samples service that I pay for).

VI. Gameplay

Mechanics- The player will move left and right to dodge the oncoming cars. Control- arrow keys Onboarding- The main menu has all of the game's controls. It is a very simple control scheme so there is little need for a full page for onboarding.

DOCUMENTATION

Process: After realizing that PixiSkater was out of scope for the time frame, I decided to take a different approach. I decided to make an infinite scroller with night driving as a center. I was planning to add a cop system which would make the game more challenging, however I was unable to add it in time before the deadline.

Art and Assets: I created all of the art and music on my own. The sound effects are pulled from a sample library service that I pay for called Splice.
